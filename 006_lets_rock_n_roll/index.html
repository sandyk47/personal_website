About

My Story

My career was forged in China’s dynamic Cross-Border eCommerce landscape, where I launched dozens of western businesses across a diverse range of eCommerce, livestreaming, search, video, livestreaming and social platforms.

These foundational experiences taught me a great deal about resilience, agility, adaptability, customer-centricity and data-oriented innovation, and were instrumental in setting me on a path to my current passion for product management.

Some of my achievements include:

  • Developing multiple multi-million-dollar omnichannel eCommerce and Smart Retail businesses in China.
  • Developing several SaaS payment solutions from ideation through to delivery, each eliminating substantial user friction and opening up new commercialisation opportunities (in the realms of hundreds of $millions in Gross Processing Value).
  • Developing a community platform for a Non-Profit; enabling scalability by streamlining operations and automating training and resource distribution.
  • Developing an automated user-onboarding tool saving 50+ days/year of manual onboarding.
  • Managing supply chain transformation companies saving employers/clients upwards of 30% p/a in shipping and warehousing costs.

I am passionate about building exceptional products that users love and that drive businesses – and the world - forward. I have a keen eye for detail, but always with the bigger picture in mind, and an innate ability to inspire teams, fostering environments centred around empathy, collaboration, personal development and growth.
